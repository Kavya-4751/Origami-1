Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons can camouflage to blend in to their surroundings
  2. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches
  3. Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  1. Cicadas can't eat, getting nutrients from tree roots
  2. Cicadas have five eyes
  3. Cicadas thrive in warm enviornments

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  1. Pandas need bamboo in their diet
  2. Their fur helps them blen into their enviornment
  3. Pandas mostly live in southwest China

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  3. Pigeons can fly at average speeds of up to 77.6 mph
